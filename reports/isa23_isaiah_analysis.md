The general becomes the particular.

Both are nation-oracles assigned a precise fixed term of years (seventy / three) with the comparative formula "like the days/years of ___." This time-stamped structure is a recurring device across Isaiah's oracles against the nations (cf. also Isa 21:16). Isaiah 23's seventy-year sentence on Tyre is built on the same template as the three-year sentence on Moab.
